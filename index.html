About the Author

Drew Neisser

Founder and CEO of Renegade, Drew is a recognized authority on cutting-edge marketing techniques, having won numerous awards for creativity and campaign effectiveness. Ranked among Brand Quarterly’s “50 Marketing Thought Leaders Over 50,” he is an “expert blogger” for Forbes, FastCompany, CMO.com, TheCMOclub and pens the highly praised CMO of the Week column for Social Media Today.

Through a long-time partnership with The CMO Club, Drew has met and interviewed well over 100 CMOs in the last five years. Drew is a trusted advisor to many of these CMOs and authors and TheCut, his coveted monthly newsletter. He regularly consults on digital and social media trends via the GLG network and currently sits on the boards of the Urban Green Council and Duke NY.
